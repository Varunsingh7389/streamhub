StreamHub
Distributed Adaptive Video Streaming Platform
Distributed video streaming platform powered by Spring Boot, Kafka, AWS S3, Redis, FFmpeg and HLS.

Supports adaptive streaming in 1080p, 720p, 480p and 360p, asynchronous video processing through Kafka, and secure content delivery using AWS S3 pre-signed URLs.
